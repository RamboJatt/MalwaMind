Study material

Learning goals

  • Recall multiplication facts up to 12 × 12.
  • Multiply numbers using partitioning or formal methods.
  • Divide whole numbers and interpret remainders.

Mini lesson

Multiplication can be broken into parts: 37 × 6 = 30 × 6 + 7 × 6 = 180 + 42 = 222. Division means sharing or grouping. A remainder is what is left over after equal groups are made.

Worked examples

  1. 46 × 7 = 322.
  2. 672 ÷ 8 = 84.
  3. 53 ÷ 6 = 8 r 5 because 6 × 8 = 48 and 5 is left.
