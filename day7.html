Study material

Learning goals

  • Review all Year 5 topics covered this week.
  • Practise choosing the right method for each question.
  • Complete the 100-question final exam after the daily revision set.

Mini lesson

For mixed questions, first identify the topic: place value, operation, fraction, decimal, measure, geometry or word problem. Underline key numbers, choose a method, calculate carefully and check your answer.

Worked examples

  1. Estimate first, then calculate exactly.
  2. For word problems, write the operation before solving.
  3. Use inverse operations to check your answer.
