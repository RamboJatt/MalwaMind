Study material

Learning goals

  • Convert between common metric units.
  • Find perimeter and area of rectangles.
  • Work with angles, time intervals and simple data.

Mini lesson

Remember key conversions: 1 m = 100 cm, 1 kg = 1,000 g, 1 L = 1,000 mL. Perimeter is the distance around a shape; area is the space inside it.

Worked examples

  1. 3 m 45 cm = 345 cm.
  2. A rectangle 8 cm by 5 cm has perimeter 26 cm and area 40 cm².
  3. Angles on a straight line add to 180°.
