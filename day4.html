Study material

Learning goals

  • Recognise equivalent fractions and simplify fractions.
  • Compare fractions and add/subtract fractions with the same denominator.
  • Find fractions of quantities and convert between improper and mixed fractions.

Mini lesson

Equivalent fractions have the same value, such as 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8. To add or subtract fractions with the same denominator, keep the denominator the same and work with the numerators.

Worked examples

  1. 3/8 + 2/8 = 5/8.
  2. 2/3 of 24 = 16 because 24 ÷ 3 = 8, and 8 × 2 = 16.
  3. 17/5 = 3 2/5.
